Location: Port Mulgrave, North East England, England (UK)

Accommodations: Other, 2 Bedrooms, baths (sleeps 4)

Beach 1 mile Terraced character cottage for 4 This traditional cottage is situated in the cliff top village of Port Mulgrave with spectacular cliff walks from the top of the lane, and a well kept mature garden to enjoy the peace and tranquility this cottage affords. Cosily furnished and equipped with a real fire to curl up in front of after lovely days out along Yorkshire's Heritage Coast. The superb beach of Runswick Bay is 1 mile, Sandsend's enormous sandy beach is 6 miles, while the picturesque fishing village of Staithes is 2 miles, and the bustling town of Whitby is 9 miles. The beautiful North Yorkshire Moors are also close by. Shop 1 mile, pub and restaurant 0.5 miles. Ground floor: Lounge/diner, kitchen, bathroom with shower over bath, toilet and basin. First floor: 2 bedrooms: 1 double, 1 twin. Electric heating, open fire in lounge (initial supply of fuel provided), all other fuel and power included in rent, bed linen and towels included in rent, colour TV, DVD player, video, washer/dryer, microwave, enclosed lawned garden, garden furniture, off road parking for 1 car.
